Frequently Asked Questions

What is yoNro?

+

yoNro is an AI-powered mental wellness ecosystem that combines wearable technology, environmental adaptation, and intelligent emotional support. Unlike traditional wellness apps that only track your state, yoNro actively transforms your environment to support your mental wellbeing in real time.

Do I need the wearable to use yoNro?

+

The Starter plan works without a wearable, offering AI therapist access and manual mood tracking. However, the Plus and Complete plans require the NeuroBand for continuous biometric monitoring and automated environmental adaptation.

Can I use it without the Smart Room Kit?

+

Absolutely. The Starter and Plus plans work perfectly without the Smart Room Kit. You'll still get AI support, analytics, and wearable integration. The Smart Room Kit is only required for the Complete Ecosystem plan if you want full environmental automation.

Is my data private and secure?

+

Yes. We use end-to-end encryption for all biometric and personal data. Your information is never sold to third parties, and you have complete control over what data is collected and stored. Privacy is a core principle, not an afterthought.

Which plan is best for me?

+

Start with Starter if you want to explore AI-guided wellness. Choose Plus if you're serious about tracking and want wearable integration. Go for Complete Ecosystem if you want the full experience with automated environment control. Most users find Plus offers the best balance of features and value.

How long is the free trial?

+

All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You can cancel anytime during the trial with no charges.
